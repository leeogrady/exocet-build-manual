Contents / Grille

Bonnet scoop
  1. Cut 2 corners back to allow snug fitting into the bonnet scoop.
  2. Mark and cut with aprx 10mm extra around the hole for scoop.
  3. Fold edges to fit against fiberglass.
  4. Remove, clean and apply a good bead of PU adhesive around the edge.
  5. Refit the grille.
  6. Apply more PU/smooth as required ensuring a good bond.
  7. Wedge/hold/tape/tie anything to keep in place while it sets.

Nose cone
  1. Hole grille inside nose cone.
  2. Mark and cut with aprx 10mm extra around the hole.
  3. Fold edges to fit against fiberglass, cutting a small wedge out for top corners is recomended.
  4. Remove, clean and apply a good bead of PU adhesive around the edge.
  5. Refit the grille.
  6. Apply more PU/smooth as required ensuring a good bond.
  7. Wedge/hold/tape/tie anything to keep in place while it sets.
