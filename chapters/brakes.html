Firstly 2 brackets are required to join the flex hose to solid brake pipe

  1. Using 3x30mm mild steel cut a piece 130mm long (30+85+15).
  2. Mark 30mm from one end (pipe connector)
  3. Mark 15mm from the other (mounting)
  4. Drill a 17mm hole in the center of the 30mm section
  5. Bend the 30mm section to 90degrees
  6. Hold the bracket up against the front sub frame (refer to pictures) in position
  7. Drill 2 6mm holes through the chassis avoiding the spot welds, maintaining enough spacing to fit bolts
  8. Mark the bracket for these holes and drill
  9. Bend this 15mm in the oppostite direction to the 30mm section to 45degrees.
  10. Paint
  11. Mount on to the subframe
  12. Repeat for second side

Fit pipe from Master cylinder to each flex pipe

  1. Route pipe from the master cylinder end port to the drivers side flex hose.
  2. Route pipe from forward brake bias output to passeger side flex hose.
  3. Clamp flex hose to bracket with spring clip

Fit pipe from Master cylinder to rear T

  1. Remove T and rotate to allow fitting with rear bulk head in position.
  2. Using the lower clip poiint on the fuel hose brackets attach the brake line.
  3. Connect to T and to master cylinder
