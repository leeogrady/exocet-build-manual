Nose Cone

Contents / Nose Cone

Important

  • Bonnet must be fitted
  • Radiator must be fitted, will need to be tilted to allow access to headlight brackets.
  1. Gently spread the sides of the nose cone enough to fit on the front of the chassis.
  2. The panel will sit quite happily with tension on the radiator, but I used some tape to the bonnet to be sure.
  3. Draw a line marking where the chassis sits behing the panel.
  4. Mark off 3 points for the bolts. I used 1, 3 & 5 inches from the rear of the cone. - Inches because the numbers looked good.
  5. Use a 6mm drill bit, make holes through the fiberglass just marking the chassis.
  6. Remove the panel
  7. Use a 9mm drill bit on the marked holes.
  8. Fit m6 rivnuts to the holes.
  9. Reinstall the nose cone, holes may have to be enlarged slightly due to angles on the chassis and rivnuts not pulling square.
  10. Mark the panel where the headlight bracket will need to go then remove the panel.
  11. Cut a slot in the panel for the headlight bracket
  12. Refit the panel, adding headlight bractets before bolts.
  13. Fit all bolts, noting that the headlight bracket bolts may need the top of the radiator tilting out of the way.
